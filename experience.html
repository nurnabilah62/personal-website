My Experience
Family Sports Day Taska Permata 2019-BA1185C

During semester 5, I was given an opportunity in handling the event.
This is because we need to learn for the subject Organizing Event(OBM310).
My classmates and I were handling Sports Day Taska Permata for the children.
The children's range age that participated in this event is between 4 years old until 6 years old.
Our event were held on October 19, 2019. Each of us were hold a position as a bureau and carried on the tasks during the event.

There are three bureau of activities and I am one of them. We are given the responsibility to determine the appropriate games
for preschool children, parents and also teachers and to ensure the activity runs smoothly. In addition, we are also responsible
for the purchase of some stuff for the games to be play during the event so that there will be no shortage of goods during the event.
We also need to make sure that the equipments for the game is adequate to avoid any problems that would drag the time set in the tentative.
While the game is running, we also have some volunteers in helping us handled the games.

For the activities, we have divided into three field so that will make easier for the bureaus to prepare for the games.
We name it “Padang A”, “Padang B” and “Padang C”. For Padang A, bureau of activities involved in controlling the field is Nurussyifa Binti Basri
which the activities that been played in the field are “kutip gula-gula”, “pecahkan belon” and “isi air dalam botol” for the kindergarten categories
while for the parents and teachers, she will control the games which are “lambung kasih” in categories of teachers and mother, and “musical chair”
for the categories of teachers and mother too.

For “Padang B”, the person in charge for control the field is Nor Aqielah Binti Kamarul Zaman. Activities that have been run on that field
for kindergarten categories are “susun menara tin”and “bawa bola pingpong” meanwhile for parents and teacher's categories
are “cari suara anak” in categories of father and kid, “lari dukung anak” for categories of father and kid,
“lambung kasih” in categories of mother and kid, and last one is “pecah belon” in categories teachers and kid.

For the last field named “Padang C”, the person in charge to control the field is Nur Nabilah Binti Hamzah.
Activities that have been run on the field for kindergarten categories are “makan biskut” and “golek kelapa”
while for parents and teacher‟s categories are “makan epal” that involve teachers and the kids, “sambut bola kecil”
that involve teachers and the kids and the last activities is “ikat kaki berpasangan” which involves in this game are the parents.

For the colouring competition, we have one person in charge for the games which is Nur Syafiqah from venue preparation bureau.
We have given complete guideline for her to follow and just collect the papers when the times is up. Overall, all activities went smoothly
with the help of a few volunteers and the activities when well because complete preparation has been made.

We are so happy for having an experience working with each other as this is our first task
working in a group, handling the game's activity and hold the position as bureaus of activity.
We are so enjoyed and feel satisfied by making the event successfully
even though it is our first event that we held.
